The four Gospels of Mark, Matthew, Luke, and John tell the story of Jesus Christ from four complementary perspectives, witnessing to his unique role in our relationship with God. This clear, accessible edition based on the ASV is designed for both individual reading and group study. If you are curious about Christianity, in the Gospels you will encounter its source. If you recently converted, the Gospels are the "pure spiritual milk" you need. If you are a long-time believer, these are "the words of eternal life" worth returning to time and again. Thanks be to God!
