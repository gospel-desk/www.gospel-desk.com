Life is a story, and God is the author. He is also the protagonist, and yet He invites me to join in. Reading the Gospels every day powerfully connects my small story with God's grand Story.

The Gospel Desk App focuses on one story each day, chosen from the Gospels of Saints Matthew, Mark, Luke, and John. The curated selections follow the lectionary of the Orthodox Church in America, a yearly cycle connecting you to thousands of years of Tradition and hundreds of millions of fellow believers around the world.

Install the Gospel Desk App, and raise your attention heavenward throughout your day by meditating on the Holy Gospel, according to the rhythms of the Church's continual pilgrimage toward God.

Let us attend!
