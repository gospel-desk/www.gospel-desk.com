A minimalist presence in a crowded genre, the Gospel Desk App offers exactly one story each day from the Gospels of Saints Matthew, Mark, Luke, and John. The curated selections follow the lectionary of the Orthodox Church in America, a yearly cycle connecting you to thousands of years of Tradition and hundreds of millions of faithful around the world. Install the Gospel Desk App, and raise your attention heavenward throughout your day, according to the rhythms of the Church's continual pilgrimage toward God. Let us attend!
